### Delete line from beginning to cursor
- `d0`, deletes from beginning
- `d^`, deletes from non-whitespace beginning

### Close splits in window
#### Keep current split only
- `:on`
- `:only`
- `ctrl-w` `ctrl-o`
- `ctrl-w` `o`
#### Close a split
- `ctrl-w` `q`
- `:q`
